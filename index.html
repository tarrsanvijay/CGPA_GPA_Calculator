How Grading Works
For Theory courses the process of grading evaluation is Relative Grading the grading depends on class average. For Lab courses Absolute Grading is followed, means the grades are fixed. Each course you study carries a certain number of credits (e.g. 1, 2, 3, 4), Grade S=10, A=9, B=8, C=7, D=6, E=5, F=0. At the end of the semester, the GPA is calculated by
GPA = Sum of all (credits X grade) / total no of credits of semester.
